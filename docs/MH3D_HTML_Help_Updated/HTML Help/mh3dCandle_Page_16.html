The final step in modeling the candle is to build the flame.

Click the Add Lock button and add 6 points in a teardrop shape near the wick. Figure 40 shows this spline.

Group the points that were just added by selecting any point along the spline and pressing the “/” key.

Click the Scale Manipulator, then move the pivot down and to the left by clicking on the center handle and dragging it with the mouse in the direction of the white arrow as shown in figure 42.
