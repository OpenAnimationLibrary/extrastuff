Lasso

A way of grouping. Lasso allows you to encircle a specific set of control points, no matter how convoluted the area you are trying to group together.

Lathe

The process of rotating a cross section around the Y axis to create a surface. Lathing is justly named after the spinning table used to make pottery because lathed objects are symmetrical and look a lot like vases.

Latitude Constraints

Restricts a Segments ability to move up and down.

Library

Refers to the collection of objects and figures stored by this software. They can be used a number of ways, as a basis for your own creations, or to be placed as is in your own scenes.

Light Cones

Lines drawn in the Direction window to demonstrate where the light falls for each light source.

Local Axis

When you choose to Translate, Scale, or Rotate a child Segment, its X, Y, Z axis will be local, meaning they are inherited

Local Coordinates

Local coordinates allow a Material to be connected to an object and move with the object.

Locking Handles

A locked handle stops movement from occurring past that point. For example, if a handle is moved that is further up the chain than a locked handle, no calculations will occur past the locked handle. If a handle is moved that is further down the chain than a locked handle, no calculations will move up past the locked handle.

Longitude Constraints

Restricts a Segments ability to move side to side.
