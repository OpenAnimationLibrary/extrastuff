It is in a Directing window that you assemble your cast of characters and tell them what to do. The cast members and their actions can be your own or carefully picked from the existing library, but your job as director does not change. You are also the cameraman, gaffer, and choreographer. Whether you are just beginning or an experienced user, you will receive much satisfaction as the pieces of your story come together here.

Initially, a new Choreography window contains a view into your world as seen from the top. Visible is a gray grid on a black background, and a purple rectangle representing the field of view of the camera.
