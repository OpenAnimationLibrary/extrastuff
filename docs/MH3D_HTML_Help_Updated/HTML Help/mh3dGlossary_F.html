Focal Length

Determines the amount of the scene the Camera can see. Smaller numbered focal lengths see larger amounts of the scene. Larger numbered focal lengths see smaller amounts of the scene.

Fractal Maps

Fractal maps are simply displacements that flat shade the facets of perturbation as if they were polygons. Fractal maps are best used when large values of deformation are required.

Frame Cut-Off

The rectangle superimposed on the screen when viewing a scene from the Camera view. Anything inside this rectangle will be rendered.

Front Projection

The process of projecting an image onto an object or into a scene.
