The “Aim At” constraint aims the pivot of the constrained bone in the direction of the pivot of the target bone. This constraint supports blending with adjacent aim at constraints.

“Aim At” is generally used to point Cameras and lights at a target bone. Also, for a character’s eyes to follow a car passing by, the “Aim At” constraint lets you specify the target bone for the “Right Eye” bone to follow, (the “Left Eye” bone is usually constrained to “Orient Like” the “Right Eye”).
