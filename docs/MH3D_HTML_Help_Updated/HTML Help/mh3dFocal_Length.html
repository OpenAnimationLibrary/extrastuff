The Focal length of the lens determines the amount of the scene the Camera can see left and right. Smaller numbered focal lengths see larger amounts of the scene: larger numbered focal lengths see smaller amounts of the scene (a typical camera zoom lens has a focal length of 80). Typical focal lengths range from 20 to 200.
