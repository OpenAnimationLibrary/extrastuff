There is always a minimum of one Camera in a scene. A wireframe representation of the camera and the camera cone are visible when you first open a Choreography window.

The purple pyramid shape drawn with its apex at the Camera is the cameras field of view. Everything within the pyramid can be seen by the Camera. The pyramid is affected by the focal length of the Camera's lens.

The Camera can be moved about the scene by dragging it with the mouse, or by adding it to a path with a path constraint.

There are many options available for the camera, which are accessed by click the camera, then making the appropriate changes on the Properties Panel.
