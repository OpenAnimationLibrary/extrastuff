Directing

Directing mode can be accessed once you have chosen to create a new Choreography, or have opened an existing one for modification by clicking the Directing mode.

Directing mode is used to place figures, lights, and the camera in to a scene and rotate or translate them over time.

Modeling, Bones, Muscle, and Skeletal modes can also be accessed from Directing mode by clicking the appropriate button.
