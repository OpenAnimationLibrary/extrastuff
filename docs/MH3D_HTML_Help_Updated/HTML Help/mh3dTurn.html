You can change the angle of view within the window, (setting the Bird’s Eye angle). Click the Turn button on the Navigation Toolbar, then press and hold the mouse button inside the appropriate window and move the mouse. The Roll and Swivel angles of the view will change.

The default shortcut key for Turn is T.
