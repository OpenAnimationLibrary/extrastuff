Camera

The Camera in MH3D operates similarly to a camera in real life.

Camera Cone

the pyramidal shape drawn in a choreography window with its apex at the Camera. The Camera can see everything within that pyramid.

Camera View

The view from the camera, which shows what the camera ‘sees’. Characters, the grid, and all paths are drawn with perspective in this view.

Capture Frame

Captures the current frame from the motion capture device.

Capture Sequence

Begins capturing frames from the selected motion capture device, starting at the current frame.

Cast Shadows

A setting that tells a bone’s geometry to cast shadows.

Channel

The means by which movement and ease is carried out. Every motion has a channel associated with it.

Checker

A Material combination method using two materials to create a checkerboard effect.

Children

Bones added to a character that are part of a hierarchy and move when their parent moves.

Choreography

The combination of all characters, camera movement, light positioning, and scene composition. Created in a Direction window.

Control Point

The individual points that make up a spline.

Cookie Cut Maps

An image map type that allows the background color of the image to be cut out. 32-bit images (24-bit images with an 8-bit alpha channel) can be used.

Curve

Drawing mode that displays a splines true outline.

Cylindrical

A projection mapping technique used to apply maps to cylindrical shapes.
