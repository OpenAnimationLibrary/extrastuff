The process of creating an object such as a cup, person, or tree, with splines is called modeling. Modeling can sometimes be time consuming, but the objects you make are durable and can be used over and over.

There are many different kinds of modelers, the most common being the polygon editor. Polygon editors are very good for designing mechanical objects since they use straight lines and sharp edges, but lack the ability to create any organic shapes or characters.

The modeler in Martin Hash

’s 3-Dimensional Animation is patch based, therefore, it is very good for creating organic shapes with curved lines and smooth edges. The modeler can also create mechanical objects just as easily as polygon modelers, since patch modelers are much more powerful than polygon modelers.
