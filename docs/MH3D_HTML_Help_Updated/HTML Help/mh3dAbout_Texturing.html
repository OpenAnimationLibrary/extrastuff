After you have finished creating your model, you will most likely want to apply some textures to it to add some realism. There are essentially three different methods for applying textures in MH3D.

The first is called Patch Coloring. Patch coloring allows you to create materials and apply them to the individual patches that make up your model. This allows a varied combination of materials over the surface of a model.

The second method utilizes the MH3D Materials Editor to create algorithmic textures that you can apply to the control points associated with bones in your models.

The final method is called Decal. Decals allow you to interactively place and paste an image directly to the surface of your model.
