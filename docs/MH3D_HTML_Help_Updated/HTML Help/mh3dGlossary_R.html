Receive Shadows

A setting that specifies that a bones geometry is to receive shadows from the objects around it.

Reflection Maps

A gray scale image used to designate differing values of reflectivity over an objects surface. White areas on the map will cause the objects surface to become 100% mirrored, and black areas will cause the object to not be reflective at all.

Relative Motion

What a character is doing regardless of time or space.

Render

Shading of the wireframe models.

Roll

Camera movement in which the camera pivots from side to side, but remains on the subject.
