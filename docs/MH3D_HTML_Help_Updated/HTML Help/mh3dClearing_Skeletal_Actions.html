The CLEAR item in the Action menu will remove all motion off of the figure. When you save over an existing Action, of course all existing Skeletal motion will be lost.

If for some reason, you wish to remove the Skeletal motion from a character, delete the Bones Channels under the action for the bones whose actions you wish to clear.
