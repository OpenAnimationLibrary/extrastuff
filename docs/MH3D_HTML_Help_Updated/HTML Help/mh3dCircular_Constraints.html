It is possible to create simple or complicated circular constraints. A simple example of a circular constraint is to translate the right shoulder to the right wrist. The shoulder can’t be positioned until the wrist is positioned, but the wrist position depends on the shoulder position. This is considered circular and can not be solved, resulting in a “Circular Constraint Detected” error message.
