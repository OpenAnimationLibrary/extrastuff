Markers

Markers are lines that can be placed in any window for your own use in orientation, whether to line up objects, to keep a character’s hand positioned in one place, or to see what pixel a particular item will be at when rendered from camera view.

Material

An algorithmic texture created with the material editor.

Material List

The list of materials on a bone.

Modeling

The creation of a model.

Motion Capture

The process of utilizing a device that enables the software to receive position and/or orientation data from an outside source and use it to position Figures.

Motion Library

A collection of Actions that are reusable on different characters, such as walking, running, or waving.

Muscle Motion

The distortion of the translation and curvature of the splines in a Segment. Muscle motion is the actual manipulation of the control points that make up the segment.
